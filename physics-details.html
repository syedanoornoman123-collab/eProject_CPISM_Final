Course Overview

This comprehensive Physics Fundamentals course is designed for beginners who want to master the basic concepts of mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism. Through engaging lectures, hands-on experiments, and practical problem-solving sessions, you'll develop a solid foundation in classical physics.

Whether you're a high school student preparing for exams, a college student needing extra support, or simply curious about how the physical world works, this course will provide you with the knowledge and skills to understand and apply fundamental physics principles in real-world situations.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand and apply Newton's Laws of Motion
  • Solve problems involving kinematics and dynamics
  • Master the principles of energy, work, and power
  • Explore thermodynamics and heat transfer
  • Understand electric and magnetic fields
  • Analyze simple circuits and electromagnetic induction
  • Apply conservation laws to physical systems
  • Develop scientific reasoning and problem-solving skills
  • Interpret and create scientific graphs and diagrams
  • Prepare for advanced physics courses and standardized tests

Prerequisites

This course is designed for absolute beginners with no prior physics knowledge required. Basic mathematics skills including algebra and geometry are recommended but not required as we'll cover the necessary mathematical concepts throughout the course.

All you need is curiosity about how the physical world works and willingness to engage with new concepts. We provide supplementary materials for students who want to brush up on foundational mathematical concepts before diving into physics principles.
