Course Overview

Bienvenue! Welcome to our French Conversation course designed specifically for intermediate learners who want to improve their speaking and listening skills. This immersive program focuses entirely on practical communication, helping you move from textbook French to natural, flowing conversation.

Through engaging dialogues, role-playing scenarios, and authentic French materials, you'll develop the confidence to express yourself clearly in various social and professional situations. Our emphasis on pronunciation, idiomatic expressions, and cultural nuances will help you sound more like a native speaker.

Prerequisites

This course is designed for students who have completed beginner-level French or have equivalent knowledge. You should be comfortable with basic French grammar, have a vocabulary of approximately 500-800 words, and be able to form simple sentences.

Ideal for students who can understand basic French but struggle with speaking fluency and natural conversation. We'll build on your existing knowledge and focus entirely on improving your communication skills.

Teaching Methodology

Our conversation-focused approach uses proven techniques:

Communicative Language Teaching: Learn through meaningful interaction
Task-Based Learning: Practice language through real-world tasks
Audio-Lingual Method: Develop automatic responses through pattern practice
Cultural Immersion: Learn language in cultural context
Personalized Correction: Individual feedback on pronunciation and fluency
Confidence Building: Safe environment to practice and make mistakes
